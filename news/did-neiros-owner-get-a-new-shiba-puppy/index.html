Did Neiro (and Formerly Kabosu’s) Owner Get a New Shiba Puppy?

by Shahzaib Ahmed
February 18, 2025 - 5:46 PM EST
in blockchain news
0
0
SHARES
0
VIEWS
Share on FacebookShare on Twitter

A quickly deleted tweet and blog post from the owner of Neiro, the Shiba Inu who succeeded the late Kabosu, have sparked speculation that a new puppy may have joined the household. The tweet, which was reportedly visible for only a few seconds, included a photo of what appeared to be a young Shiba Inu with the caption:

ブログを更新しました🆙

外に出ると、 小さなハルが楽しそうに走り回っていました 風を感じて、草の匂いをかいで、 まるで世界が宝物みたい！💓

Please check out the new blog post😊

Translated: "I updated my blog 🆙

When I stepped outside, Little Haru was joyfully running around Feeling the wind, sniffing the grass, as if the whole world was a treasure! 💓

PIease check out the new blog post😊"

Shortly after the tweet was deleted, a blog post featuring the same caption and a similar photo also disappeared. This has left many wondering whether this was an accidental reveal, an early teaser, or something else entirely.

Neiro was introduced last summer following the passing of Kabosu, the original Doge meme dog. Since then, Neiro has taken on a special place in the hearts of fans, carrying on the legacy of the Shiba Inu that helped shape internet culture. The mention of “Little Haru” suggests that Neiro may now have a younger companion, though without further confirmation, it remains speculation.

Why Were the Posts Deleted? The quick removal of both the tweet and blog post raises a few possibilities:

An Accidental Reveal: The tweet and blog post may have been published prematurely before the owner was ready to make an official announcement.

A Future Introduction: If Haru is a new addition, there may be plans for a more formal introduction at a later time.

A Possible Misinterpretation: While the caption refers to “Little Haru,” it’s unclear whether this means a new puppy has joined the household or if the name refers to something else entirely.

For now, there is no official word on whether Neiro has a new sibling. The fact that both the tweet and blog post were deleted suggests that either the timing was unintentional, or the posts were never meant to be public at all. If the tweet was indeed an early glimpse of a new puppy, fans may get a proper introduction soon. Until then, the brief appearance of "Little Haru" leaves the door open for curiosity and anticipation.
